Frequently Asked Questions

The AI suggested a part that doesn't fit. What do I do?
Our AI is a design architect meant for inspiration. Always verify technical dimensions (like stem heights or case diameters) with the specific parts supplier before purchasing.
What movements does the app support?
Currently, our AI is optimized for the Seiko NH series (NH34, NH35, NH36, NH38, NH70) and the 7S26/4R36 calibers.
How do I report a bug?
Please email us with your device model and a screenshot of the issue. We appreciate your help in making Seikomod Achitect better!
